Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Welcome to NordicWings ("we", "us", or "our"). We operate the website nordicwings.net and the NordicWings Android app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the core features of NordicWings. However, we may collect the following automatically:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, search queries entered, links clicked, and time spent on pages — collected anonymously via Google Analytics.
  • Device data: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and language preference.
  • IP address: Used for geographic analytics (country/city level) and security purposes. We do not store or sell IP addresses.
  • Cookies: Small files placed on your device to remember preferences and measure traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve NordicWings
  • To understand how visitors use our site and app
  • To display relevant flight and hotel deals
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services & Affiliate Links

NordicWings contains affiliate links to third-party booking platforms including but not limited to: Kiwi.com, Trip.com, Jetradar, Booking.com, Agoda, Expedia, Hotels.com, and 12Go Asia. When you click these links and make a booking, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

These third-party services have their own Privacy Policies. We encourage you to review them before making any booking. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

4. Google Analytics & Advertising

We use Google Analytics to understand site traffic. Google may use cookies to track your activity across websites. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.

We participate in the Google AdSense program. Google may show ads based on your browsing history. You can manage ad personalisation at adssettings.google.com.

5. Travelpayouts & Affiliate Networks

We use Travelpayouts and other affiliate networks to track referral bookings. These services may set cookies on your device when you click our affiliate links to attribute any resulting booking to our account. No personally identifiable information is shared with us through these networks.

6. Data Retention

We retain anonymised analytics data for up to 26 months. We do not store personal booking data — all bookings are processed entirely on the third-party platform you are redirected to.

7. Children's Privacy

NordicWings is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
