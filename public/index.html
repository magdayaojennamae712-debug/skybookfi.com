🎫 Ticket and check-in

Your booking reference is shown on the confirmation screen immediately after payment. Please save or screenshot this reference. Contact us at support@nordicwings.net with your booking reference and we will send your e-ticket to your email.

Check-in is done directly with the airline, not through NordicWings. Visit the airline's official website and enter your booking reference (PNR) and last name. Most airlines open online check-in 24–48 hours before departure.

Baggage allowance depends on the fare type. If you need to add extra baggage, you can do so directly on the airline's website using your booking reference after purchase. We are working on adding in-app baggage options soon.

Finnish citizens and EU residents do not need a visa for the Philippines for stays up to 30 days. You need a valid passport (at least 6 months validity beyond your travel dates) and a return or onward ticket. Always check the latest entry requirements with the Philippine embassy before travelling.

Cancellation and change policies depend on the airline and fare type. Most economy fares are non-refundable or have a change fee. Contact us at support@nordicwings.net as soon as possible and we will help you with the options available for your booking.

💸 Cancellations & Refunds

NordicWings is a flight search and comparison service — we do not process bookings or payments directly. All bookings are made on the airline's or partner site's own platform. To cancel, please contact the airline or booking site where you completed your purchase. Your confirmation email will have their cancellation contact details.

Cancellation deadlines are set by the airline or booking partner, not by NordicWings. The deadline depends on the fare type you chose at the time of booking. Check your confirmation email or the airline's website for the specific rules that apply to your ticket.

Whether you receive a full or partial refund depends entirely on the fare rules set by the airline or partner site where you booked. NordicWings does not handle payments or refunds. Common fare types:

  • Flexible / refundable fares — usually eligible for a full refund.
  • Standard economy fares — often only taxes are refundable.
  • Promotional / sale fares — typically non-refundable.

Contact the airline or booking platform directly for your specific case.

Refund timing is handled entirely by the airline or booking partner where you purchased your ticket. NordicWings does not process payments. Typically, refunds take 5–30 business days depending on the airline and your bank. Check with the partner site for an accurate estimate.

If the airline cancels your flight, you are entitled to a full refund under EU Regulation 261/2004 (for flights to/from the EU). Contact the airline directly using the details in your confirmation email to claim your refund or request a rebooking. NordicWings does not process refunds on behalf of airlines or partners.

Date changes are handled directly by the airline or booking partner where you made your purchase. Check your confirmation email for their contact details or visit their website. Change fees and availability depend on the fare rules of your specific ticket — NordicWings is not able to modify bookings on your behalf.
