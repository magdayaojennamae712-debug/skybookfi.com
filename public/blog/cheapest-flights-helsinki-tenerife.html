Tips to Get the Cheapest Helsinki–Tenerife Flights

  • Book winter flights early – December, January and February flights from Helsinki to Tenerife sell out months in advance.
  • May and October are hidden gems – excellent weather (24°C) and the lowest prices of the year.
  • Compare charter vs. scheduled flights – TUI and Aurinkomatkat offer all-inclusive packages that can work out cheaper than booking flights and hotels separately.
  • Tenerife South (TFS) is better than North (TFN) for beach holidays – TFS is closer to Playa de las Americas and Los Cristianos.
  • Fly mid-week for savings of up to €30 compared to weekend departures.

Why Tenerife is Perfect for Finnish Travellers

Tenerife enjoys year-round sunshine with average temperatures of 22–28°C. During the Finnish winter when temperatures in Helsinki drop to -10°C or below, Tenerife offers a welcome escape with warm sea temperatures, beautiful black sand beaches at Playa Jardín and the iconic Mount Teide volcano.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the flight from Helsinki to Tenerife?

Direct charter flights take approximately 5 hours 30 minutes. With one connection the journey takes 7–9 hours depending on the layover.

What is the cheapest month to fly from Helsinki to Tenerife?

May and October offer the best combination of low prices and good weather. Avoid December, January and February when demand from Finnish winter sun seekers pushes prices up.
