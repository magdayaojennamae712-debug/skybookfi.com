Cheapest Flights Helsinki to Philippines in Winter 2026

📅 May 11, 2026 ⏱ 6 min read ✍ NordicWings

Winter in Finland means dark days and temperatures below -20°C. For many Filipinos living in Finland and Finnish travellers seeking sun, flying to the Philippines in December, January or February is the perfect escape. The Philippines has warm, dry weather during these months — ideal for beaches, family visits and island hopping.

Here's everything you need to know about finding the cheapest Helsinki to Philippines winter flights in 2026.

Best Airlines for Helsinki–Philippines Winter Flights

  • Emirates (via Dubai) — Most popular, reliable, great onboard service. Often the cheapest option when booked early. From €420 return.
  • Qatar Airways (via Doha) — Excellent comfort, 5-star service, short layover in Doha. From €440 return.
  • Turkish Airlines (via Istanbul) — Budget-friendly option with free stopover in Istanbul if you want to explore. From €430 return.
  • Finnair + Cebu Pacific — Good for Cebu and Davao connections via Manila. From €460 return.
💡 Smart Tip — Fly January Not December

If your schedule allows, January flights cost €200–€300 less than December flights. The Philippines weather is equally beautiful in January — it's the perfect month to visit. Book by October for best prices.

How to Get the Cheapest Winter Flights to Philippines

  1. Book 3–4 months ahead for Christmas travel, or 6–8 weeks for January/February
  2. Be flexible by 1–2 days — prices can vary by €100+ on different dates
  3. Avoid Dec 20–Jan 5 peak period if possible — prices double
  4. Check Tuesday/Wednesday departures — typically cheapest days to fly
  5. Compare all airlines on NordicWings — one free search shows all options
  6. Set a price alert — when a sale drops, you'll be ready to book

What's the Weather Like in Philippines in Winter?

The Philippines has dry season from November to May — perfect timing for winter visitors from Finland! Manila is around 28–32°C, Cebu and Davao are similar. Beach destinations like Palawan, Boracay and Siargao are at their best during this period.
