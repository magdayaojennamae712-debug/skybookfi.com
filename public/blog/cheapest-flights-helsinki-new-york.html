Helsinki to New York: What to Expect

The direct Finnair flight takes approximately 9 hours 30 minutes from Helsinki to New York JFK. With a connection, total travel time is typically 11–14 hours depending on the layover.

New York is served by three major airports: JFK (John F. Kennedy), Newark (EWR) and LaGuardia (LGA). Most international flights arrive at JFK or EWR. Both are well connected to central Manhattan by public transport.

Tips to Get the Cheapest Helsinki–New York Flights

  • Book 2–3 months in advance for summer travel and 6–8 weeks ahead for winter.
  • Fly mid-week (Tuesday or Wednesday) – fares are often €30–€60 cheaper than weekends.
  • Consider flying to Newark (EWR) instead of JFK – it is sometimes €40–€80 cheaper.
  • Avoid school holidays – Finnish summer holidays (June–August) and US Thanksgiving push prices up significantly.
  • Set a price alert on NordicWings and get notified when fares drop.
  • January and February are the cheapest months to fly, with fares regularly under €380.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a direct flight from Helsinki to New York?

Yes. Finnair operates a direct (non-stop) flight from Helsinki HEL to New York JFK. The flight takes approximately 9 hours 30 minutes westbound.

What is the cheapest month to fly from Helsinki to New York?

January and February are consistently the cheapest months, with fares from around 350 euros. Avoid June, July and August when prices are at their peak.

How long is the flight from Helsinki to New York?

The direct Finnair flight takes about 9 hours 30 minutes. With one stop, the total journey is typically 11–14 hours.
