Where Are Finns Flying This Summer? 8 Hottest Destinations from Helsinki 2026

📅 Published May 5, 2026 ⏱ 6 min read ✈ Helsinki (HEL) departures

Summer 2026 flight searches are in full swing — and the data is clear. Finns are searching for sun, warmth and affordable city breaks more than ever before. We analyzed the most-searched flight routes from Helsinki Vantaa Airport this season and picked the 8 hottest destinations you should know about right now.

Whether you want guaranteed sunshine on a Spanish beach, a buzzing Asian city, or a quick weekend in Scandinavia, this list has something for every type of traveler — and every budget.

📊 How we chose these destinations

Based on flight search volume data, Google Trends for Finnish travelers, and newly announced routes for summer 2026. Prices shown are typical one-way economy fares from Helsinki (HEL) — book in advance for the best deals.

🔥 The 8 Hottest Flight Destinations from Helsinki Right Now

🇪🇸 #1 — Málaga, Spain 🔥 #1 Most Searched From €80–150

Málaga tops the flight search charts from Helsinki this year — and it's easy to see why. The Costa del Sol offers guaranteed sunshine (300+ days per year), stunning beaches, easy access to Marbella and Nerja, and a buzzing food scene in the city itself. Direct flights from HEL make it one of the most convenient sun escapes in Europe.

✈ ~4h direct from HEL ☀️ 26–32°C in summer 🏖️ Costa del Sol beaches 🍷 Excellent local food

🇹🇭 #2 — Bangkok, Thailand 🔥 Top Asia From €350–600

Bangkok is consistently one of the most searched long-haul destinations from Helsinki. The combination of incredible street food, vibrant nightlife, luxury hotels at European budget prices, and easy connections to Thai islands (Koh Samui, Phuket, Koh Lanta) makes it irresistible. Finnair flies direct to Bangkok Suvarnabhumi — one of the best long-haul deals out of Helsinki.

✈ ~9h direct on Finnair 🌡️ 28–35°C year-round 🍜 World-famous street food 🏝️ Islands nearby
Search HEL → Bangkok on NordicWings →
🇳🇱 #3 — Amsterdam, Netherlands From €70–140

Amsterdam never goes out of style. From the iconic canals and world-class museums (Rijksmuseum, Van Gogh Museum, Anne Frank House) to the lively nightlife and incredible cycling culture — it's a city that rewards every type of traveler. Multiple airlines fly HEL→AMS daily, keeping prices competitive. It's also a great gateway for onward connections into Western Europe.

✈ ~3h from HEL 🚲 Best cycling city in Europe 🎨 World-class museums 🏨 Hotels from €80/night
Search HEL → Amsterdam on NordicWings →
🇩🇰 #4 — Copenhagen, Denmark From €50–120

Copenhagen is the most searched Nordic destination from Helsinki — and a perennial favourite for a reason. The Danish capital combines beautiful architecture, Michelin-starred restaurants (it's the world's food capital for a reason), Tivoli Gardens, and a relaxed Scandinavian atmosphere. It's only a short hop from Helsinki, making it perfect for a long weekend. SAS and Finnair compete heavily on this route, keeping fares low.

✈ ~2h from HEL 🍽️ World-class dining 🎡 Tivoli Gardens 🚴 Bike-friendly city
Search HEL → Copenhagen on NordicWings →
🇮🇹 #5 — Catania, Sicily 🆕 New Finnair Route! From €90–180

Finnair launched a brand-new direct route from Helsinki to Catania (Sicily) this summer — and searches have spiked immediately. Sicily is one of Italy's most beautiful and underrated destinations: Mount Etna, ancient Greek temples, black-sand beaches, and some of the best food in the Mediterranean. This new direct connection makes it easier than ever to explore Southern Italy from Finland.

✈ ~4h direct on Finnair 🌋 Mount Etna volcano 🏛️ Greek temples 🍋 Fresh Sicilian food
Search HEL → Catania on NordicWings →
🇬🇧 #6 — London, United Kingdom From €60–150

London is one of the most-flown routes from Helsinki year-round — and summer 2026 is no exception. With 390 flights per day between Helsinki and London, competition is fierce and fares are often very competitive. Whether you're going for the museums, theatre, shopping or just the iconic experience of one of the world's great cities, London delivers. Fly into Heathrow, Gatwick or even Stansted for the cheapest fares.

✈ ~3h from HEL 🏰 World-class attractions 🎭 West End theatre 🛍️ Oxford Street shopping
Search HEL → London on NordicWings →
🇵🇱 #7 — Warsaw, Poland 💶 Best Value From €40–90

Warsaw is consistently one of the cheapest destinations to fly to from Helsinki — and it massively over-delivers for a budget city break. The Old Town (rebuilt after WWII) is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, the food scene is incredible and affordable, and the nightlife rivals Berlin at a fraction of the price. LOT Polish Airlines and Finnair keep competition strong on this route. Warsaw Modlin (WMI) is often even cheaper via Ryanair.

✈ ~2h from HEL 🏰 UNESCO Old Town 🍺 Budget nightlife 🥩 Pierogi & local food
Search HEL → Warsaw on NordicWings →
🇸🇪 #8 — Stockholm, Sweden From €40–110

Stockholm is the single busiest flight route from Helsinki — with over 416 flights per day across all operators. It's the go-to Nordic city break: stunning archipelago scenery, great restaurants, ABBA Museum, Gamla Stan (Old Town), and excellent design and fashion shopping. Easy to combine with a ferry trip via the Tallink Silja Line for a scenic Baltic experience.

✈ ~1h from HEL 🎵 ABBA Museum 🏝️ Archipelago islands 🛳️ Ferry option too

📅 When Should You Book for Summer 2026?

The data shows that the ideal booking window for Finnish summer travel is 60–70 days in advance. That means if you're planning to fly in July or August, you should be booking right now in May.

  • Book now (May): Best selection, best prices for July–August peak season
  • June departures: Still some good deals available — act fast
  • Last-minute (less than 3 weeks): Prices spike 40–60% above the average on popular summer routes
  • Mid-week flights (Tuesday/Wednesday): Typically 15–25% cheaper than Friday/Saturday departures
  • Early morning departures: Often the cheapest time slot on any given day
⚠️ Summer 2026 demand is high

Flight search volumes are up significantly compared to 2025. Popular routes like HEL→Málaga and HEL→Bangkok are filling up fast. If you see a good price — book it. Waiting rarely helps in a busy summer market.
