London is one of the most popular short-haul destinations from Helsinki. The flight takes just 3 hours, making it perfect for a long weekend break. With multiple airlines competing on this route, you can often find one-way fares from as low as €49. Here is everything you need to know about cheap Helsinki to London flights in 2026.

💡 Airport tip: London has 5 airports. Heathrow (LHR) is most central; Gatwick (LGW) is well connected by train; Stansted (STN) is furthest from central London but Ryanair fares are often the cheapest. Always factor in the cost of transport to central London when comparing prices.

Tips to Get the Cheapest Helsinki–London Flights

  • Book Ryanair to Stansted for the cheapest fares – but budget €20–25 for the Stansted Express train into central London.
  • January and February are the cheapest months for Helsinki–London flights.
  • Book 4–8 weeks in advance for short-haul European routes like this one.
  • Fly mid-week – Tuesday and Wednesday flights are often €15–€30 cheaper.
  • Check both LHR and LGW – sometimes Gatwick is much cheaper than Heathrow for the same dates.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the flight from Helsinki to London?

The direct flight from Helsinki to London takes approximately 3 hours. All major airlines operate non-stop services.

Which London airport is cheapest from Helsinki?

Ryanair flights to London Stansted (STN) are usually the cheapest. However, always compare the total cost including transport to central London when choosing between airports.

What is the cheapest month to fly from Helsinki to London?

January and February consistently offer the lowest fares, often under 60 euros one-way. July is typically the most expensive month.
